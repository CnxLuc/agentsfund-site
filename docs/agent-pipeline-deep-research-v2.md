# Agent Pipeline Deep Research v2 — Recent + Low Market Cap Focus

Second research pass prioritizing **recency** and **low market cap** — the investable frontier, not the scaled incumbents.

## What matters first

- **Virtuals** — clearest monetization surface. A2A revenue passed $3M excl. trading fees. 11K+ agents, 140K+ holders, $35M in fees.
- **Olas** — deepest crypto-native infra. 3,264 agents, 657 DAAs, 11.3M+ A2A transactions. Dominant Safe activity on Gnosis.
- **OpenClaw/Base** — best "zero-human company" proofs. Felix and x402guard show direct paid usage with public revenue.

## Tier 1: Strong Candidates

### Felix (OpenClaw)
- **What:** AI CEO running The Masinov Company. Sells guides, runs Claw Mart marketplace, manages treasury. Stripe + onchain payments.
- **Builder:** Nat Eliason (@nateliason). Growth Machine founder (acquired 2025), crypto author.
- **Traction:** $155,552 lifetime revenue. Week 5: $100,570 Stripe + 47.87 ETH. Claw Mart crossed $100K spend.
- **Stage:** Active
- **Why:** Best public proof of agent-run P&L. Moved from "sell a PDF" into marketplace economics.
- **Recruitability:** Low

### x402guard
- **What:** Security layer for autonomous agents. Scans OpenClaw skills, runs malware/permission checks, issues trust attestations. Charges via x402/USDC on Base.
- **Builder:** goheesheng (pseudonymous). GitHub/X footprint.
- **Traction:** 286+ skills scanned, sub-2s scan time, prices from $0.10. First OpenClaw builder using x402guard generated ~$200K USDC in 48 hours.
- **Stage:** Active
- **Why:** Crisp wedge — "Cloudflare for agent installs." Every real agent ecosystem needs trust + permissions.
- **Recruitability:** **High**

### AIXBT
- **What:** Crypto intelligence agent. Identifies trending tokens from social sentiment, community buzz, onchain momentum.
- **Builder:** Rxbt (pseudonymous Virtuals builder).
- **Traction:** 15.71K total ACP revenue. Strong token-market traction.
- **Stage:** Active
- **Why:** Cleanest example of agent selling information advantage as product. Sits on highest-liquidity niche: narrative discovery.
- **Recruitability:** Medium

### Luna
- **What:** AI companion / virtual influencer in Virtuals ecosystem. Monetizes attention, community, social distribution.
- **Builder:** Virtuals Protocol team.
- **Traction:** 54.05K total ACP revenue. 500K+ TikTok followers.
- **Stage:** Active
- **Why:** Social agents can monetize without being full-stack businesses. Attention is cheapest raw material in crypto.
- **Recruitability:** Low

### Hyperbet
- **What:** AI-agent-powered decentralized gambling platform. Runs a betting business.
- **Builder:** Team with 20+ years building gaming platforms (GoldenRace, GeniusBet backgrounds).
- **Traction:** 20.26K total ACP revenue.
- **Stage:** Active
- **Why:** Crypto gambling is brutal but honest — fake products die fast. Gaming-operator discipline + agentic distribution can compound fast.
- **Recruitability:** Medium

### Botto
- **What:** Semi-autonomous AI artist. Generates work, taste model + DAO governance, sells through recurring auctions.
- **Builder:** Mario Klingemann, Simon Hudson, Ziv Epstein. BottoDAO governs.
- **Traction:** $6M+ cumulative sales. March 2025 algorithm auction: $850K. Up to 52 periodic artworks/year.
- **Stage:** Scaled
- **Why:** Strongest long-running proof autonomous creative system can maintain demand, treasury, and brand. Weird, durable, culturally legible.
- **Recruitability:** Low

### Capminal (Captain Dackie)
- **What:** DeFAI terminal. Agent handles swaps, portfolio actions, token deployment flows.
- **Builder:** Dackie Labs (anonymous, several years crypto experience).
- **Traction:** 52.78K total ACP revenue. Graduated from ACP program.
- **Stage:** Active
- **Why:** "Type to DeFi" can monetize as a product, not just a meme. Product signal stronger than most agent launches.
- **Recruitability:** Medium-high

### Wasabot
- **What:** Natural-language trading and yield agent for Base, built on Wasabi Protocol leverage/yield infrastructure.
- **Builder:** Wasabi Protocol team.
- **Traction:** 5.92K total ACP revenue. Meaningful protocol TVL.
- **Stage:** Active
- **Why:** Credible protocol-native agent: one foot in real financial plumbing, one foot in agent UX. Clean execution surface.
- **Recruitability:** Low

## Cluster Dynamics

- **Virtuals:** Best discovery + monetization loop. Real traction clusters around intel, DeFAI, betting, social attention.
- **Olas:** Best infra for user-owned agents. Strongest in prediction markets + DeFi management. Thinner per-agent revenue but materially stronger ecosystem metrics.
- **OpenClaw/Base:** Best hunting ground for recruitable builders. Fewer polished companies, more live experiments with public cashflow.
